About Vaani Setu

Understanding the silence and the mission behind the voice.

Our Solution

Millions of individuals in the deaf and mute community rely on sign language, but a significant communication barrier exists with the non-signing world. This gap can lead to social isolation, limited access to education, and fewer employment opportunities.

How It Works

User Signs
AI Interprets
Text/Speech Output

Why We're Building This

We believe in a world where everyone has a voice. Our mission is to leverage technology not just for innovation, but for empathy and inclusion. Vaani Setu is our commitment to building bridges, fostering understanding, and empowering every individual to be heard.

Features

Packed with technology to make communication accessible to all.

Real-Time Recognition

Our AI model interprets ISL gestures directly from your device's camera feed.

Text & Speech Output

Instantly convert gestures into clear, readable text and natural-sounding speech.

Two-Way Translation

Communicate seamlessly with future support for voice-to-sign translation.

Web Accessible

No downloads needed. Access Vaani Setu from any browser on any device.

Multilingual Support

Future-proofed to support multiple regional languages and sign language variations.

Lightweight & Fast

Designed to run efficiently on mobile devices without draining your battery.

Our Roadmap

The journey ahead to a more inclusive world.

0-6 Months

Crack Indian Sign Language

Develop and refine the core AI model for high-accuracy ISL recognition.

6-12 Months

Expand Globally

Begin research and data collection for other major sign languages (e.g., ASL, BSL).

12-18 Months

Add Voice-to-Sign AI

Build the two-way communication module, converting spoken language into AI-generated sign avatars.

Beyond

Become the World's Bridge

Establish Vaani Setu as the global standard for sign language communication technology.
